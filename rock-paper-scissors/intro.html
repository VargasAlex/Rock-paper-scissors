Before you play..

*For the best experience please set your browser to full screen!

The game is Rock, paper, Scissors. If it's your first time playing, please go to this link: Click Me!
There you can learn the game basics. Now, this game follows the same rules but with a few additions.
To make the game more fun of course.

Here are the rules:

  • When you hit play, you will be brought to the battlefield. You start on the the left hand side
    where you will see images clickable images to chose your pick.
  • Once you click an image, the computer will randomly chose their pick.
  • On the top of the page there is a scoreboard which will update the scores for both sides.
  • If you win a round, you will gain 2 points.
  • If you lose a round, the computer will gain 2 points
  • If you and the computer tie, no points will be gained on either side.
  • The first one to reach 10 points, wins the game.
  • Good luck and have fun!
